Spaghetti Carbonara

This is a Spaghetti Carbonara recipe

Description

Spaghetti Carbonara recipe

Ingredients

  • spaghetti of choice
  • eggs
  • butter
  • parmegano cheese
  • pasta water
  • black pepper

Steps

  1. boil the spaghetti ad leave 50 mil pasta water
  2. mix all ingredients in a bowl
  3. with the pasta in the pan ad off the stove stir in the egg mixture
  4. stirr untill it thickens
  5. add some cheese on top
