This is a Pesto Pasta recipe

Description

Pesto Pasta recipe

Ingredients

  • spaghetti of choice
  • pesto
  • olive oil
  • parmegano cheese
  • pasta water

Steps

  1. boil the spaghetti ad leave 50 mil pasta water
  2. after boiling add the pesto to the pan with the spaghetti
  3. with the pasta in the pan ad off the stove stir it in
  4. add some cheese on top
